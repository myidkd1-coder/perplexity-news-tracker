## Full Article
Brazilian President Luiz Inácio Lula da Silva will visit India from February 18–22, leading the largest-ever Brazilian delegation. Over 300 business leaders and 12 cabinet ministers will accompany him. Lula will attend the India AI Impact Summit 2026 and hold bilateral talks with Prime Minister Narendra Modi on February 21.The visit focuses on trade, defence, energy, digital public infrastructure, AI, rare earths and Global South cooperation.
