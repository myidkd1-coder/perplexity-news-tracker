Lovable Raises $330M in Series B Funding to Power No-Code AI Platform

Lovable, a Swedish AI startup, announced a $330 million Series B round at a $6.6 billion valuation. This new infusion will help Lovable scale its app-building tools and meet surging demand for no-code AI solutions in both consumer and corporate settings.
