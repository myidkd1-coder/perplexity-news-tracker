## Full Article
Prime Minister Narendra Modi will inaugurate the India AI Impact Expo 2026 at Bharat Mandapam in New Delhi on February 16. The Expo, held alongside the India AI Impact Summit from February 16–20, will span 10 arenas across 70,000 sq metres, feature 13 country pavilions, over 300 exhibitions, 600+ startups and 500 sessions with 3,250+ speakers. Under the IndiaAI Mission, the government will unveil 12 indigenous foundation models tailored to India’s linguistic diversity, positioning the country as a key AI innovation hub in the Global South.
