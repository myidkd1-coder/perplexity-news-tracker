## Summary
Researchers analyzing large pediatric datasets report that serious neuropsychiatric events such as seizures and hallucinations were linked to influenza infection itself rather than to the antiviral drug oseltamivir (Tamiflu). The findings suggest Tamiflu does not significantly increase these risks in children, addressing longstanding safety concerns and supporting its continued use for flu treatment.
