Reform Agenda Focuses on Easing Business

The Union Budget 2026-27 is shaping up as a significant step in India's reform trajectory, with an intensified focus on deregulation across various sectors. Policymakers are prioritizing the simplification of business operations, making deregulation and decriminalisation the cornerstones of the fiscal plan for FY27.

Compliance Overhaul on the Horizon

A primary objective involves streamlining registration and approval processes for both companies and individuals. The Finance Ministry is actively assessing recommendations from multiple government departments aimed at cutting through existing red tape. Businesses expecting significant relief may see routine inspections scaled back, transitioning to a more targeted, technology-driven, risk-based compliance model.

Decriminalisation of Lapses

Criminal action is expected to be reserved strictly for grave offenses. Most minor procedural shortcomings, long a source of business friction, are slated for decriminalisation. This broad agenda aims to unlock greater productivity and enhance overall economic efficiency across the nation.
