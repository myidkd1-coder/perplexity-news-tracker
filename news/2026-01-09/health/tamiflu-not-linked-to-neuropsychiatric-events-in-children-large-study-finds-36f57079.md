## Summary
A long-running safety debate over the flu drug oseltamivir (Tamiflu) in children may be resolved by new research indicating that serious neuropsychiatric events, including seizures and hallucinations, are associated with influenza infection itself rather than the medication. The findings support continued pediatric use of Tamiflu when clinically indicated.
