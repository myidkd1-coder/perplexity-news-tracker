## Summary
The United Nations has raised India’s 2026 growth projection to 6.6%, citing resilient private consumption, robust public investment and supportive policy measures such as tax cuts and GST rationalisation. The assessment acknowledges a drag from steep new US tariffs on Indian exports but says demand from Europe and West Asia will partly cushion the impact while inflation is seen at 4.1% in 2026.
