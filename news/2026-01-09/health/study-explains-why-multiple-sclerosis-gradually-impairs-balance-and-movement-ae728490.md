## Summary
Scientists have found that inflammation in the brains of people with multiple sclerosis disrupts the energy supply to key neurons that control movement and coordination. As these neurons lose energy, their function deteriorates over time, helping explain the progressive loss of balance and mobility seen in many MS patients and pointing to new treatment targets.
