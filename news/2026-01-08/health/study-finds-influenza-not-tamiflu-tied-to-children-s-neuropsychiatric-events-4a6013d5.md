## Summary
Researchers report that serious neuropsychiatric symptoms such as seizures and hallucinations in children with flu are linked to the infection itself rather than the antiviral oseltamivir (Tamiflu). The findings may reassure clinicians and parents about the medication’s safety while underscoring the dangers of severe influenza.
